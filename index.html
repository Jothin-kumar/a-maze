How to play?

  • Find the green dot in the maze. This will be the starting position
  • Find the red dot in the maze. This will be the final position
  • Start navigating to the final position

Navigation

  • Devices with physical keyboard: Use arrow keys or WASD
  • Touch screen devices: Maze can be scrolled. Use onscreen navigation buttons
* On touch devices, press and hold to reset onscreen navigation buttons

Common problems

  • Visibility issues: Disable your dark reader browser extension
